Refund Policy

Returns, exchanges, and refunds

Returns & Exchanges

If you're not happy with a purchase, you can request a return or exchange within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unused, unworn, and in its original packaging.

Refunds

Once a returned item is received and inspected, refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Non-Returnable Items

Items marked as final sale, or products damaged through misuse, are not eligible for return.
